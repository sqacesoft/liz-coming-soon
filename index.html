What is LiZ.fun?

LiZ.fun is a friendly launchpad on Solana that helps anyone introduce community tokens in minutes. Our focus is transparency, simplicity, and supporting good causes.

Who is it for?

Creators, communities, and supporters who want a simple, fun way to rally around ideas and causes—without needing to learn complex crypto tools.

Projects can dedicate a share of their activity to donations. We highlight donation‑friendly projects so supporters can easily find and follow them.
